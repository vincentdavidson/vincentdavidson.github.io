About Me

Vincent Davidson is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, where he serves as a Graduate Research Assistant. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Clemson University.


His research focuses on trustworthy and context-aware Large Language Models (LLMs), knowledge graphs, scalable AI systems, and human-centered machine learning.


Vincent currently serves as Specialist, Databricks Engineer at Nationwide Headquarters, where he builds enterprise-scale machine learning and data engineering solutions.
