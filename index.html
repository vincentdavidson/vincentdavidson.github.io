Vincent Davidson

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, where I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Clemson University and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Winston-Salem State University.

My research focuses on trustworthy and context-aware Large Language Models, knowledge graphs, scalable AI systems, and human-centered machine learning.

I currently serve as Specialist, Databricks Engineer at Nationwide Headquarters, where I build enterprise-scale machine learning and data engineering solutions.
